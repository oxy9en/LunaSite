Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025 • Effective Date: November 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Luna Alarm ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS mobile application Luna Alarm ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

During onboarding, we collect the following information to personalize your AI assistant experience:

  • Name
  • Preferred pronouns (He/Him, She/Her, They/Them)
  • Preferred language
  • Chosen virtual avatar

You may also provide optional information through interactions with the AI assistant.

1.2 Usage Data

We may collect certain information automatically:

  • App usage activity (e.g., alarms created, time settings)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Interaction logs with the AI assistant (non-sensitive, anonymized)
  • Crash logs and performance metrics

1.3 Alarm-Related Data

To provide the core functionality, the App stores:

  • Alarm schedules (time, days of week)
  • AI avatar settings

This data is stored locally on your device and/or in cloud backups if enabled by iOS.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide alarm and wake-up features
  • Personalize interactions with the AI assistant
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Maintain app security
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. AI Assistant Interactions

All AI-generated responses are automated. Depending on your settings, anonymized interaction data may be sent to AI processing services solely to generate responses.

We do not use your name, pronouns, or profile data for targeting, advertising, or external analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party tools (such as analytics or AI processing services). These services may collect limited technical data necessary for functionality.

We ensure all partners comply with applicable data protection laws.

5. Data Retention

  • Profile data is stored until you delete the App or reset your profile.
  • Alarm settings remain on the device until removed.
  • Usage analytics (if any) is retained only as necessary for operational purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is fully secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Luna Alarm is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Opt out of analytics
  • Request corrections

Contact us at support@lunaalarm.app to submit a request.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates take effect upon posting within the App.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
